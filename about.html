This project provides fans of Parks and Rec easy access to memorable quotes from Ron Swanson.

We use the Ron Swanson Quotes API to retrieve quotes and display them in an easy-to-read format.

Ron Swanson is an iconic character known for his dry humor and love for meat and woodworking. Our application pays homage to this beloved character.
