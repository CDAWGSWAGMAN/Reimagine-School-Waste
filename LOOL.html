Why is food recovery important?

Food recovery is essential because it helps reduce environmental impact by preventing food waste from contributing to landfills and greenhouse gas emissions. It also plays a critical role in addressing hunger by redirecting surplus food to those in need, supporting vulnerable communities. Economically, food recovery saves money for businesses and households and increases the efficiency of the food system. Ethically, it is a way to balance the abundance of food in some areas with the scarcity in others. Overall, food recovery fosters a more sustainable, equitable, and responsible approach to food management.
