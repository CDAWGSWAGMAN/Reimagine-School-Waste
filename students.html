Students

Start by referring to the toolkit and garner interest with fellow classmates. Determine if your school has an environmental club or green team. When approaching a principal it is more effective when multiple student make the request. If your school does not have a green team leader identify a staff member who might be willing to support a new program. Remember that you are the constituent, every staff member in you school including the principal has a job because of students. Student voices are very powerful and the request can be presented in the form a of a demand.
